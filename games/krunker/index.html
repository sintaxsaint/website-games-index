2. Movement (Slide Hop)

Scroll wheel jumping may not work in browser. The best way to move fast is Slide Hopping:

  • Jump (Space) -> Move Forward
  • Hold Shift (Crouch) while in air
  • Land, then immediately Jump again.

This is the pro technique for speed.
